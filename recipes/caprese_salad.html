Caprese Salad


Description:

A vibrant and visually appealing Caprese Salad is a celebration of fresh flavors and colors. This classic Italian dish combines ripe, juicy tomatoes with creamy mozzarella cheese and aromatic basil leaves. Each slice of tomato and mozzarella is carefully arranged, creating a delightful mosaic of red, white, and green hues on a serving plate. The freshness of the ingredients is accentuated by a drizzle of high-quality extra-virgin olive oil, enhancing their natural flavors. Optionally, a touch of balsamic glaze lends a sweet and tangy note to the salad. The dish bursts with the simplicity of its components, offering a burst of summer in every bite, making it an ideal starter or side that delights both the eyes and the palate.

Ingredients:

  • 2 large ripe tomatoes
  • 1 ball of fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Balsamic glaze (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash and slice the tomatoes and fresh mozzarella cheese into 1/4-inch thick slices.
  2. Arrange the tomato and mozzarella slices alternately on a serving plate.
  3. Tuck fresh basil leaves in between the tomato and cheese slices.
  4. Drizzle extra-virgin olive oil over the arranged slices.
  5. Optionally, drizzle balsamic glaze over the salad for added flavor.
  6. Sprinkle salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve immediately as a refreshing appetizer or side dish.
