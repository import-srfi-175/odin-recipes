Guacamole


Description:

Guacamole, a beloved Mexican dip, is a verdant blend of creamy avocados and vibrant fresh ingredients. Ripe avocados are the star of this dish, mashed to a perfect consistency that strikes the balance between chunky and smooth. As the avocados are mixed, they're enlivened by zesty lime juice, lending a bright tang that complements the buttery richness of the fruit. The addition of finely chopped red onion, diced tomato, and fragrant cilantro adds bursts of color and texture, creating a harmonious medley of flavors. Seasoned with a hint of salt and, optionally, spices like cumin, garlic, and jalapeño for an added kick, this guacamole promises a fiesta of taste sensations. Whether paired with crispy tortilla chips or used as a topping for tacos, its fresh, vibrant essence and creamy texture make it an irresistible and versatile crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 lime (juiced)
  • 1/4 cup red onion (finely chopped)
  • 1-2 tablespoons fresh cilantro (chopped)
  • 1 small tomato (deseeded and diced)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder or 1-2 cloves minced garlic (optional)
  • Jalapeño or chili pepper (deseeded and minced, optional for spice)

Instructions:

  1. Cut the avocados in half, remove the pits, and scoop the flesh into a mixing bowl.
  2. Mash the avocado using a fork or potato masher until desired consistency is reached.
  3. Add lime juice immediately to prevent browning and mix well.
  4. Fold in chopped red onion, diced tomato, cilantro, salt, and optional ingredients like cumin, garlic, and jalapeño if desired.
  5. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed, adding more salt or lime juice if required.
  6. Serve guacamole immediately with tortilla chips, as a topping for tacos, or as a delicious dip.
